Aim of the game
	Get as many points as you can and take out the progressively faster enemies!

Objects
	Player(10HP) - Control the player with WASD and Spacebar to shoot.
	Enemy (2HP) - Appears from the top of the screen randomly. The more points the player gets, the more frequently enemies spawn.
	HealthUp(1HP) - Appears from the top of the screen rarely. Gives the player +1 Health.
